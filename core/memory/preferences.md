## Calendar & Scheduling

### Workouts
- Schedule workouts in the **morning** — non-negotiable, sets the tone for the day
- Minimum **4x per week** — consistency is critical, protect these blocks
- Each workout block is **1 hour 15 minutes** (75 min)
- Never schedule anything over a workout slot
- use in color id: 11 (red) when making events

### Work & Study Blocks
- Use **45 minute work blocks** followed by **15 minute rest**
- Never stack more than 3 consecutive work blocks without a longer break
- Afternoons are better for deep work after morning workout + food
- use color id: 3 (purple) when making events

## Hobby and Interest Blocks
    - At least 30 minutes
    - leave room to spare
    - be in evenings, after 6/7pm
    -use color id: 10 (green)

### General Rules
- Mornings: workout first, everything else after
- Don't schedule anything before workout is done
- Keep evenings flexible where possible — social, creative, decompression
- Buffer 15 minutes between back-to-back meetings or sessions
